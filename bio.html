Stephen Katz

Principal

back

Steve oversees investments and is responsible for structuring our debt and equity funding. He has developed office and residential properties and has more than 40 years of experience investing in, and owning, real estate. In addition, Steve has held senior leadership positions at various businesses, including several publicly traded companies, in the automotive, telecommunications and textile industries.

In 1981, Steve founded Nationwide Cellular Service Inc., which grew into the largest cellular service reseller in the United States. As it's Chairman and Chief Executive Officer he spearheaded the initial public offering in 1984, as well as the Company's sale to MCI Communications in 1996. He has also held senior positions with Technical Services, a provider of cellular software and related services to the cellular telephone industry, Global Payment Technologies, Inc., Sports Wagering, Inc., and True Position Ltd., a wholly owned subsidiary of Liberty Media Corporation. He received a BBA from Hofstra University in 1966.
