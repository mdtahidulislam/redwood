Redwood Real Estate Group is a fourth-generation real estate investment, management, and development company based in Manhattan. We operate property across the United States with a focus in New York City, New Jersey, Long Island, Pennsylvania, Westchester, and Connecticut.

Our investment strategy is guided by generations of acquisition, development, and improvement of urban properties. We typically invest in assets with an identifiable value proposition, which generally means prospects for improving returns through redesign and repositioning. We also acquire select fully stabilized properties that generate an attractive risk-adjusted return.

We participate in a wide variety of sub-sectors within real estate including multi-family, mixed-use, retail, office, and industrial. We often work with tenants and partners to identify and develop property. Our trophy development is a 32-story high-rise condominium designed by Philip Johnson on the Upper East Side in Manhattan.
